Teatime Safety Standards

Teatime is a women-only platform designed to provide a safe, respectful, and supportive environment. We strictly prohibit harassment, exploitation, or any harmful activity. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and comply with all applicable international child safety policies.

Our Safety Commitments

  • All users must verify their identity using live selfie authentication before accessing the platform.
  • We prohibit and take action against harassment, exploitation, abusive content, and impersonation.
  • We do not allow child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) content. Violations are reported to relevant authorities.
  • Users who act with harmful intent will be suspended or permanently removed from the platform.
  • Personal data is encrypted in transit and stored securely in compliance with best practices.

Reporting & Support

If you encounter abusive behavior, harmful content, or suspicious activity on Teatime, please report it immediately to our support team. Contact us at: teamtimesomalia@gmail.com

Enforcement

Teatime reserves the right to suspend, restrict, or permanently remove users who violate these safety standards. We are committed to fostering a secure community where women can prioritize their well-being and safety.
